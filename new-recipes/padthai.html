Ingredients

  • 1 package Pad Thai Rice Noodle
  • 1 block Firm of Extra Firm Tofu
  • 2 tb Vegetable Oil
  • 6 Green Onion Stalks
  • 2 c Bean Sprouts
  • 4 Limes
  • 6 tb Tamarind Concentrate or Paste
  • 1/4 c Pineapple Juice
  • 2 tb Rice Vinegar
  • 1/4 c Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 c Sugar (preferably palm or brown, but white is fine)
  • 2 tb Sriracha
  • 1/2 c Roasted Peanuts

Steps

  1. Cook rice noodles according to packaging.
  2. Combine 2 tb lime juice from squeezed lime, tamarind, pineapple juice, soy sauce, sugar, and sriracha to make sauce.
  3. Squeeze excess water from tofu and pat dry with paper towels, then cut into 1 inch cubes.
  4. Slice green onion in half and remove roots. Take the end closer to the roots and chop into 2 inch slices on a bias. Take the other end and cut into thin circles (this will be for garnish).
  5. Cut remaining limes into quarters.
  6. Finely chop or crush peanuts
  7. Fry tofu in Wok or Skillet on medium high heat until browned. Then, reduce heat to medium and add the long slices of green onion. Let wilt for a moment and then add half of the quantity (1 cup) of bean sprouts.
  8. Reduce to low and add the sauce and then the cooked rice noodles. Mix thoroughly.
  9. Garnish with raw bean sprouts, chopped peanuts, thinly sliced raw green onion, and lime quarters.
