Funeral Potatoes

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 bag Frozen Hashbrowns (thawed)
  • 1 bag Shredded Vegan Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 1/3 c Vegan Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 container Vegan Sour Cream
  • 2 c Cornflakes
  • 1/4 c Vegan Butter (melted)

Steps

  1. Combine hashbrowns, cheese, soup, and sour cream in a 9x16 casserole dish. Mix thoroughly.
  2. Mix together cornflakes and butter. Put into a ziploc and crush if desired.
  3. Spread an even layer of the cornflake mixture across the top of the potatoes in the casserole dish.
  4. Bake at 350 for 40-50 minutes.
